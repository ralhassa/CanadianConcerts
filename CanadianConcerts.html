Canadian Concerts Database

Originally, our group considered making an application geared towards customers interested in attending concerts. After much thought, we determined that there were already so many applications like this already on the market. After much deliberation, we decided to shift our focus from customers to concert workers and create a more behind-the-scenes type of application. Our application will display information like performer name, band name, performer id, venue, address, V.I.P available, venue capacity, number of employees working, parking available etc. This information will help employees plan and manage their concerts effectively. Our goal for our application is to help make concerts run as smoothly as possible. We know how much we enjoy attending concerts, and we hope that our application will aid in making concerts as awesome as possible.
